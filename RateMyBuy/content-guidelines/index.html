Content Guidelines

Deutsche Version

Last updated: 23.11.25

Welcome to RateMyBuy

These content guidelines define which content is allowed in our app. By using RateMyBuy, you agree to comply with these guidelines.

1. Allowed Content

  • Honest and authentic product reviews
  • Constructive criticism and feedback
  • Helpful tips and experiences
  • Respectful expressions of opinions
  • Factual discussions about posts

2. Prohibited Content

  • False or misleading information
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Spam or repeated identical reviews
  • Advertising or commercial content without proper disclosure
  • Copyrighted content without permission
  • Harassment or bullying of other users
  • Content of a sexual or violent nature
  • Fake reviews or paid reviews

3. Reviews

  • Be honest and fair in your reviews
  • Use appropriate language
  • Respect other opinions
  • No personal attacks on other users or companies

4. Photos and Media

  • Upload only your own photos or those you have rights to
  • No photos containing personal information of third parties
  • No manipulated or misleading images

5. Community Behavior

  • Respect other users
  • No insults or personal attacks
  • Constructive discussions are encouraged
  • Report inappropriate content
  • Do not share personal data of other users

6. Consequences

If you violate these guidelines, we may:

  • Remove or edit content
  • Temporarily suspend user accounts
  • Permanently close user accounts
  • Take legal action in severe cases

7. Reporting Violations

If you discover content that violates these guidelines, you can report it through the reporting function in the app. We carefully review all reports and take appropriate action.

8. Changes to the Guidelines

We reserve the right to change these content guidelines at any time. Important changes will be communicated to you via the app. The current version is always available in the app under settings.
